### Social Media Checklist (5 Do's & 5 Don'ts)

## Do's:

1. Use privacy settings to control who sees your posts.


2. Think before you post—would you want a future employer to see this? 


3. Give credit when sharing someone else's work or ideas.


4. Use professional language when interacting with faculty or industry experts.


5. Report cyberbullying or harassment if you witness it.



## Don'ts:

1. Share your password or sensitive personal information publicly.


2. Post offensive, discriminatory, or disrespectful content.


3. Tag others in photos without their explicit permission.


4. Engage with "trolls" or participate in online arguments.


5. Believe or share unverified news (fake news) without checking sources.

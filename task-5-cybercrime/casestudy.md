## Case Study: UPI/Online Payment Fraud

UPI fraud is a type of cybercrime where attackers trick victims into authorizing unauthorized transactions using Unified Payments Interface apps. It typically begins with a "phishing" message or call where the attacker poses as a bank official or a buyer on a marketplace.4

### Step-by-Step Process:

1. The attacker sends a "Request Money" link to the victim instead of a "Send Money" link.

2. The victim is told they need to enter their UPI PIN to "receive" a cashback or payment.

3. Once the PIN is entered, the money is instantly debited from the victim's account.


College students are frequently targeted due to their high usage of digital payment apps for canteen and hostel expenses. The consequences include immediate financial loss and potential identity theft if the attacker gains further access to banking credentials.

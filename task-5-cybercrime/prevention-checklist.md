## Stay Safe Online Checklist


1. Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money; a PIN is only required for sending money.

2. Verify the recipient's name on the UPI app before confirming any transaction.

3. Use 2nd Factor Authentication (2FA) on all social media and email accounts.

4. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or entering sensitive passwords.

5. Check for "https://" and the lock icon before entering data on any website.

6. Regularly update your phone’s OS and banking apps to patch security holes.

7. Do not click on "Limited Time Offer" links received via SMS or WhatsApp.

8. Report any incident immediately to the National Cyber Crime Portal at cybercrime.gov.in or call the 24x7 helpline at 1930.
